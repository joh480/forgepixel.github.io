About Me

Hi, I’m John T. Matimba — a creative web developer and digital brand builder based in South Africa. I founded Forge Pixel to help individuals and businesses craft sharp, meaningful online experiences. With a blend of storytelling, design, and technical skills, I turn ideas into websites that are bold, responsive, and unforgettable.

Skills & Expertise

  • HTML / CSS / JavaScript: Creating clean, interactive, and responsive layouts
  • Creative Design & Branding: Building visuals that speak your brand’s language
  • Website Troubleshooting: Diagnosing layout issues and optimizing functionality
  • Interactive Elements: Animations, hover effects, and navigation styling
  • Logo Design: Crafting unique, professional icons and typographic styles
  • Custom Navigation & Layouts: Seamless user journeys with sharp aesthetics
  • Writing for Web: Clear and engaging copy that boosts your site’s identity
